ABOUT US

Wildpacks is a reliable and trustworthy backpack company based in Vancouver, BC that manufactures and sells the highest quality utilitarian backpacks for the explorer. Aside from selling backpacks, we also offer expert advice and also host community events such as hiking events, photography walks, etc. At Wildpacks, we aim to be transparent with our customers and allow them to learn what goes into making the bags. Products showcase all the features in the bag in order to help customers make the most informed purchase.
