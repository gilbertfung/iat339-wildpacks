WildPacks Style Guide

WildPacks is a reliable and trustworthy backpack company that curates and sells the highest quality utilitarian backpacks for the explorer. Aside from selling backpacks, they also offer expert advice and hosts community events such as hiking events, camping events,photography walks to help showcase their products. On the website, customers are given the option to request a trial at an upcoming commmunity event. This not only helps build the brand tribe and community, but also allows customers to test out the backpacks in context.Products showcase all the features in the bag in order to help customers make the most informed purchase.

Target Audience

The young explorer and adventurer that seeks functionality, trendiness, and durability in their backpacks.

Visual Style

Calm. Mountainous. Adventurous.
